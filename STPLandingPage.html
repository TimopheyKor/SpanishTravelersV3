Welcome to Artist Travelers.

This interactive mapping companion to Americans in Spain: Painting and Travel, 1820-1920 invites you to experience the places that artists visited during their journeys to Spain and the images they created. Simply zoom in, explore the historical maps, and click on a location to begin your own journey.

This site brings together the Americans in Spain exhibition and the Spanish Travelers project, initiated and developed at Marquette University together with MarVL (Marquette Visualization Lab) and the American Geographical Society Library.
